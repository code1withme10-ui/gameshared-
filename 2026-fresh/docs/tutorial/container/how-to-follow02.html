How to Follow This Tutorial

Step-by-step professional workflow

Step 1: Prepare Your Environment

  • Create a new folder: /var/www/tutorial/containers/
  • Do NOT use your existing work sandbox
  • Place all tutorial files inside this folder
This tutorial must be completely isolated from production or client work.

Step 2: Reset Your AI Session

Before starting the exercise:

  • Log out of your AI account
  • Log back in
  • Start a brand new chat session
  • Do NOT attach the chat to any existing project
This is critical. Previous chat history may influence AI responses and break tutorial constraints.

Step 3: Initialize the AI Correctly

Download the following files:

  • Download prompt.md
  • Download SOP.md

In a brand new AI chat session:

  1. Open prompt.md
  2. Copy all contents
  3. Paste into AI
  4. Send
  5. Then open SOP.md
  6. Copy all contents
  7. Paste into the same AI chat
  8. Send
Professionals control their tools. The SOP prevents AI from drifting into complexity. The SOP constrains the AI. Professional developers control their tools.

Step 4: Follow Phases Strictly

This tutorial is divided into phases:

  • Phase 1 – Hard-coded implementation (feel the pain)
  • Phase 2 – Introduce an interface
  • Phase 3 – Introduce a container
  • Phase 4 – Extend with WhatsApp
Do not skip phases. Architectural understanding comes from progression.

How to Work With AI Professionally

Do not ask vague questions like:

“Build me a notification system.”

Instead, be precise:

“We are in Phase 1. Hard-coded version only. Do not introduce interfaces yet.”

If AI introduces:

  • Frameworks
  • Namespaces
  • Autoloaders
  • Advanced design patterns

Remind it of the SOP constraints.

Professional Conduct

  • Write code yourself
  • Read every line generated by AI
  • Understand before copying
  • Refactor manually when instructed

You are not here to generate code. You are here to understand architecture.

Professionals control tools. Tools do not control professionals.
