2. Background & Identity

Brief history:
Prosperity Early Learning Centre was established in 2015/04/15 to provide a safe, nurturing, and educational environment for young children while supporting working parents in the community.

Vision:
To be a leading early childhood development centre that nurtures confident, happy, and independent children.

Mission:
To provide quality care and early education through play-based learning in a safe and loving environment.
Core Values:
Care and safety
Respect
Learning through play
Inclusivity
Honesty

=======================================================================================================================================================================
3. About Us

Description:
Prosperity Early Leaning Centre is a safe and loving space where children learn, play, and grow. We focus on early childhood development through fun, structured activities that support emotional, social, and cognitive growth.

What makes it unique:
We offer small class sizes, personalized attention, and a caring environment that feels like a second home.
