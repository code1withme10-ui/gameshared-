 Creche Website - Home page.

1.1 Purpose
The Home Page shall serve as the primary entry point of the crèche website, providing visitors with an overview of the crèche, its services, and clear navigation to key sections.
_________


1.2 Stakeholders
Parents / Guardians (Primary Users)
Parents
Crèche Management
Website Administrator

_________


1.3 Functional Requirements
FR-HOME-01: Display Crèche Branding
The system shall display the crèche logo.
The system shall display the crèche name and tagline.
The system shall display brand colours consistently.

FR-HOME-02: Hero Section
The system shall display a hero banner with:
A welcoming headline
A short introductory message
At least one call-to-action (CTA) button (e.g., “Apply Now”, “Contact Us”)

FR-HOME-03: Navigation Menu
The system shall display a navigation menu linking to:
Home
About Us
Programs / Classes
Admissions
Gallery
Contact Us

FR-HOME-04: Overview of Services
The system shall display a brief summary of:
Age groups accepted
Educational programs
Operating hours

FR-HOME-05: Key Highlights
The system shall display key selling points, such as:
Qualified teachers
Safe learning environment
Learning approach

FR-HOME-06: Call to Action (CTA)
The system shall provide CTA buttons that redirect users to:
Online application form
Contact page

FR-HOME-07: Announcements / Notices (Optional)
The system shall display important announcements (e.g., enrolment open).

_________

1.4 Non-Functional Requirements
The page shall load within 3 seconds on standard internet connections.
The page shall be responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
The page shall be accessible in compliance with basic accessibility standards (readable fonts, contrast).

1.5 Assumptions & Constraints
Content shall be provided by the crèche management.
Images must comply with child privacy regulations.
