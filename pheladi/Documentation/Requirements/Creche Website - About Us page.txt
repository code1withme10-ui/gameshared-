Creche Website - About Us page

1.1 Purpose
The About Us page shall provide detailed information about the crèche, its values, mission, and background to build trust with parents.

1.2 Stakeholders
Parents / Guardians
Prospective Parents
Crèche Management

1.3 Functional Requirements
FR-ABOUT-01: Crèche Background
The system shall display a brief history of the crèche.
The system shall display the year of establishment.

FR-ABOUT-02: Vision and Mission
The system shall display the crèche’s vision statement.
The system shall display the crèche’s mission statement.

FR-ABOUT-03: Values
The system shall display the core values of the crèche.

FR-ABOUT-04: Management and Staff Overview
The system shall display:
Head teacher / principal details
Staff qualifications summary (no sensitive personal data)

FR-ABOUT-05: Facilities Information
The system shall display information about:
Classrooms
Play areas
Safety measures

FR-ABOUT-06: Accreditation and Compliance
The system shall display registration or compliance information (e.g., Department of Social Development).

___________

1.4 Non-Functional Requirements
Content shall be easy to read and professionally written.
The page shall support future content updates.
The page shall be consistent with branding and layout used on the Home Page.

1.5 Business Rules
All published content must be approved by crèche management.
No child personal data shall be displayed.

1.6 Dependencies
Availability of approved content from stakeholders.
Availability of images and certification documents.

1.7 Vision and Mission
Vsion: "Empowering young minds to explore, create and become confident leaders of tomorrow"
Mission: "Providing a safe, nurturing environment where little one's learn, grow and thrive"
