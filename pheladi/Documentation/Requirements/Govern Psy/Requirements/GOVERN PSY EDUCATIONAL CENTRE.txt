Historic brief
"Govern Psy Educational centre, situated in Mpumalanga was established in 2019 with humble beginnings of just five children in a church. What started as a small initiative had grown into an impactful and a dedicated learning centre with a an aim of nurturing young minds.

"We welcome children starting from the age of 2 years through  to grade 3, providing a safe, structured and stimulating learning environment. Our programs are designed to support early childhood development and strong foundational education."
