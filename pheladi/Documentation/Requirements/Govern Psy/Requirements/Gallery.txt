
Creche Website - Gallery page

1. Purpose
The purpose of the gallery page is to showcase the crèche’s facilities, activities, and learning environment in order to:
Build trust with parents/guardians.
Demonstrate daily activities and safety standards.
Promote transparency and professionalism.
 
_________

2. Functional Requirements
FR-GAL-01: Display Image Gallery
The system shall display a collection of images related to the crèche.
Images shall be organised in a grid or tiled layout.

FR-GAL-02: Image Categories
The system shall allow images to be grouped into categories such as:
Classrooms
Outdoor Play Area
Learning Activities
Events & Celebrations
Facilities

FR-GAL-03: Image Preview (Lightbox)
The system shall allow users to click on an image to view it in an enlarged (popup/lightbox) view.
Users shall be able to navigate between images while in preview mode.

FR-GAL-04: Image Descriptions
The system shall support optional captions or short descriptions for each image.

FR-GAL-05: Admin Image Management
The system shall allow authorised users (Admin/Headmaster) to:
Upload images
Edit image captions
Delete images
Assign images to categories

__________

3. Non-Functional Requirements
NFR-GAL-01: Performance
The gallery page shall load within 3 seconds on a standard internet connection.
 Images shall be optimised to reduce loading time.

NFR-GAL-02: Usability
The gallery shall be easy to navigate for non-technical users.
The interface shall be intuitive and visually appealing.

NFR-GAL-03: Responsiveness
The gallery page shall be fully responsive and usable on:
Desktop
Tablet
Mobile devices

NFR-GAL-04: Security & Privacy
Only authorised users shall be able to upload or manage images.
Images of children shall be displayed in accordance with parental consent policies.

NFR-GAL-05: Accessibility
Images shall include alternative text (alt text) for accessibility purposes.

__________

4.Approval

This document serves as the formal requirements baseline for the development of the Headmaster Page of the Crèche Website.
